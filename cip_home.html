Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know so you can use Client Intelligence Platform like a pro.

What is Asset Under Management (AUM) ?

Assets Under Management (AUM) is the total market value of assets that a financial institution manages on behalf of investors. It's a key metric used to assess the performance of a fund or portfolio.

What is FX Exposure ?

FX exposure of a fund refers to the potential risk a fund faces due to fluctuations in foreign exchange rates.

What is currency hedging ?

Currency hedging is a strategy that helps investors and businesses reduce the risk of currency fluctuations. It's used to protect against losses that could arise from changes in exchange rates.

What is pooled investment ?

A pooled investment is a fund that combines money from many investors into a single investment. The fund is then managed by a professional and the money is invested in a variety of assets.

What is global equity holding of a fund ?

It refers to the total value of all the stocks (equities) that the fund owns in companies located across the globe, meaning it invests in companies listed on stock exchanges worldwide, not just within a single country.

What is notional value of a Derivative ?

It refers to the theoretical or face value of a financial instrument, like a derivative contract, used to calculate payments on that instrument.
