Overview

Instituion

The People's Pension is one of the UK's largest independent master trusts, managing £31 billion in pension savings for over 6.8 million members as of January 2024. This significant scale positions it as a major player in the UK pension market.

The fund is set to embark on a new investment strategy in 2025, targeting up to £4 billion in private market investments by 2030. This initiative will focus initially on infrastructure and real estate assets, with a substantial portion potentially deployed in the UK. Currently, 14% of members' savings in the growth stage default fund are invested in UK-based assets, demonstrating the fund's commitment to the domestic economy.

People's Pension has also made significant strides in responsible investment. In a recent move, they shifted £15 billion into climate-aware strategies as part of their net zero commitment. This change has immediately reduced the carbon intensity of their main investment fund by 30%, with plans for further annual reductions of 7% to align with reaching net zero by 2050.
